Project Vivaan

Project Overview:

Feb 2022 - March 2023

➣ A project as a Senior Design Engineer in association with Team SHUNYA (IIT Bombay) for Solar Decathlon 2023 competition organized by US Department of Energy.
➣ Project Vivaan aims to build Net Positive Energy, Net zero water, Net zero Carbon House.
➣ One of 16 teams approved for Build Phase 2023 with prize funding of USD 50000

Role and Responsibilities:

As part of Electrical Subsystem, worked on:
➣ Automation of Cooling, Ventilation, Humidity, Hot water and Lighting systems
➣ Coordinated between Electrical and Mechanical Subsystem to automate the HVAC and Hot water system
➣ Design, documentation and testing of the automation systems

Results and Outcomes:

➣ Represented India as part of Team SHUNYA & secured 2nd place in “Solar Decathlon” organized by US Department of Energy
➣ Successfully built and tested all the Automation systems

Key Skills:

➣ Arduino, ESP, C, C++, MS Office, Actuators, Sensors, Automation
